QGIS Workshop

June 27, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Welcome!

New Maps Plus at the University of Kentucky Department of Geography is excited to offer this QGIS workshop. The workshop is completely full and we will post more details here.

Boyd Shearer is the instructor.

What you need to bring?

This workshop will provide the computer, data, and instructional material. You don't need any special software or device. However, you are welcome to bring your laptop, though we will NOT spend time installing QGIS and will focus on the Windows version.

Time allowing, you can share your data and questions at the end of the workshop. Your data should be downloadable or on a portable drive. The instructor will be avaialable after class to discuss your data.

Location and parking

The workshop is held in the University of Kentucky White Hall CB rooms 311 and 313. The nearest visitor parking is available in Parking Stucture #5, which is shown on the above map and in the official Campus map. The rate is $2/hour with a $16 maximum.

Metered on-street parking is available, but not likely will for the entire duration of the workshop. If you are willing to walk and the weather is nice, neighborhoods to the northeast don't charge for on-street parking. That could be a mile walk, however. Campus neighborhoods often require parking badges to park on street.

Lunch and snacks

A break for lunch is between noon and 1 PM. Campus has a lot of great resturants for you to find lunch. Snacks are available vending machines in the basement of White Hall

No food or open beverages are allowed at the computer stations. The lab has a classroom (shown in the below 360° photo) where one can enjoy snacks.
